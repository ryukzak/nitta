data DataflowSt tag v tp Source #

Dataflow option (tp ~ TimeConstraint t) or decision (tp Z Interval t) statement. Describe sending data between processor units over a network. Any DataflowSt has implicently linked NITTA.Model.Problems.Endpoint.
